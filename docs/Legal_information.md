

About Us:
Executive Summary
CinnAstra Tech is a specialized training and career development platform focused on the software testing and quality assurance (QA) sector. The program is designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements by offering a curriculum that spans from foundational manual testing to advanced AI-powered automation. Central to the organization’s value proposition is a "mentorship-first" approach led by industry veterans with 15 to 18+ years of experience. Key differentiators include mandated real-world internships and comprehensive interview preparation—both integrated into the standard training package—to facilitate immediate placement for freshers, career switchers, and returning professionals.

Fundamentals of testing
 Automation Testing Tracks
 Specialized and Future-Ready Testing – Cloud and AI Testing


Why chose us?
CinnAstra Tech is built on a mentorship-first learning model designed to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real industry expectations. Our programs combine deep practical training, real-world internships, and structured interview preparation to ensure learners transition smoothly into professional QA roles. With guidance from seasoned industry experts and exposure to modern automation, cloud, and AI-powered testing technologies, students gain the skills and confidence required to succeed in today’s fast-evolving software quality landscape.
